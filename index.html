My Story

From Empty Pockets
to Full Purpose

I grew up knowing hunger — not just for food, but for something better. My father worked until his final years just to keep us afloat. When he passed away, I was 14. I went to school hungry and came home to silence. But somewhere in that hardship, I found a quiet determination that never left me.

By 2011, I had finally earned my degree — working days, studying nights. I checked my bank account. $3. I took a leap of faith, joined the property industry, and told God: help me, or let me be. He did more than help. I became the 22nd top agent at Huttons, a trainer, and eventually found my home at PropNex — where I now lead a team of remarkable people, including million-dollar producers.

Today, I get to do what once felt impossible — provide for my mother, explore the world, and walk alongside families on one of the most significant journeys of their lives. Every client I serve is a reminder of how far trust, faith, and preparation can take you.
